# DPC (Dipeptide Composition) - Matematiƒçke formule

## Osnovne formule

Za peptidnu sekvencu duljine $L$:

### Ukupan broj dipeptida

$$N = L - 1$$

### DPC vektor

$$\text{DPC}(i) = \frac{\text{count}(\text{dipeptide}_i)}{L - 1}$$

gdje:
- $i \in \{1, 2, \ldots, 400\}$
- $\dim(\text{DPC}) = 400$ ($20 \times 20$ aminokiselina)
